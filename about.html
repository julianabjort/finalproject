About Tate

Hi there! My name is Tate Mckenzie and I’m a high school art teacher living in Pacific Northwest. Aside from working with students who inspire me daily, I work as an independent artist, creating a wide range of products available with my artwork prints or even custom art, where I design individualistic tattoo designs. At 26 years old I spend my free time hiking, kayaking, playing with my dog and adventures in Washington, basically anything with nature, I love it! I have always been inspired by nature. I have been so lucky to be able to travel and live in some of the most beautiful places. From Arizona to Pennsylvania to New Zealand to Europe to the Pacific Northwest, I am ALWAYS in awe of this beautiful planet, which I believe is represented in my artwork.
